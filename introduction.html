Introduction to my game
This is a Showcase to the Game that i have been working on for the last few months. It has taken alot of time and effort to create and i am happy to share it with you. I hope you enjoy!!! My game is an infinite wave survival game and you have to kill the enemies to survive the longest time. I got inspiration from various different games like the Last of Us, PT and many more. You can look at some of the images that inspired my game on the next page. I also have got a gallery of my game on the different assets i used to create it. For example, I show the main menu, the death screen as well as the UI of the game. My gallery also shows the different rooms and a quick peak of the enemy NPCs.
